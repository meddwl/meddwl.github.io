About

I’m an applied AI and computational drug discovery specialist with over six years of experience building and validating data-driven workflows for pharmaceutical and biotech research. My work focuses on turning complex chemical and biological data into reliable, experimentally relevant insights. I’ve designed and benchmarked cheminformatics and machine-learning pipelines across multiple therapeutic targets, working with large-scale datasets, structure-based modeling, and molecular dynamics. I specialize in validating models against real-world performance, comparing them to existing methods, and identifying where AI genuinely adds value. I work best with teams that need both technical depth and clear communication — whether as a short-term expert, a fractional team member, or a scientific bridge between data scientists, chemists, and decision-makers. My goal is always the same: reduce uncertainty, improve prioritization, and support confident scientific decisions.
